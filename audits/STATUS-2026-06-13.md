### MECE G7 (activity-detector cooldown on failure) - FIXED + regression test (2026-06-13, commit 0a79c44)
The audited G7 defect was in activity_detector._trigger_pattern: _recently_triggered.add()
ran AFTER `await injector.handle_trigger()` inside the try, so on injector failure the
except swallowed the error and NO cooldown was recorded -> the pattern re-fired every
detection cycle, unbounded. NOT touched by the injector change above; surfaced by external
audit. Fixed by moving cooldown registration into a `finally` (records cooldown/backoff
regardless of success). Regression test added: test_trigger_pattern_records_cooldown_on_injector_failure
(verified to fail on the pre-fix code, pass after).

### Obsidian read-sync - MECE G3 (read side) - FIXED (2026-06-13, commits 857aa64/07282a7) - was 2 failures
